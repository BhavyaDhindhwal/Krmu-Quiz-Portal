About KRMU Quiz Portal

The KRMU Quiz Portal is a modern online examination platform developed for students and administrators of K.R. Mangalam University. Students can register, attempt quizzes, view results, and download certificates instantly. Administrators can manage subjects, questions, students, results and certificates from one dashboard.
